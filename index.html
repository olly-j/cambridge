Thursday, 20 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Arrival & Morning Exploration

Arrive in Cambridge and begin your adventure with an inspiring morning walk through its charming streets.

Explore the city on foot as you soak in the vibrant atmosphere and historic architecture. This leisurely start sets the stage for a day full of discovery and inspiration.

12:00 PM - Explore Trinity College

Wander through the storied halls and lush courtyards of Trinity College.

Trinity College is a cornerstone of Cambridge’s academic heritage. Discover its rich history and impressive architecture while learning about its notable alumni and traditions.

1:00 PM - Lunch at Mercado Central (Booked)

Savor a delicious Spanish lunch at Mercado Central in a vibrant, bustling setting.

Enjoy the authentic flavors and lively ambiance that make Mercado Central a culinary gem in Cambridge. Learn about the unique dishes and ingredients that set this venue apart.

3:00 PM - Check-In at Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Relax and refresh as you settle into your elegant accommodation.

Experience modern comfort blended with historic charm. The Graduate offers an inspiring setting to unwind and prepare for an afternoon full of exploration.

4:30 PM - Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

Explore centuries of art and history at the world-renowned Fitzwilliam Museum.

Discover diverse collections ranging from ancient artifacts to modern masterpieces. The museum provides deep insights into art, culture, and the human experience.

6:00 PM - Stroll Along The Backs

Enjoy a scenic walk along the River Cam and admire the picturesque college gardens.

The Backs offer a stunning fusion of nature and history. Learn about the centuries-old colleges that line the river and take in breathtaking views along the way.

7:30 PM - Evening Leisure Walk

Wind down your day with a reflective stroll through Cambridge’s softly lit streets.

Experience the enchanting transformation of the city at dusk. This peaceful walk invites quiet reflection and a chance to savor the magic of Cambridge.

Friday, 21 Feb 2025

9:00 AM - Sedgwick Museum

Discover Earth’s ancient history through fascinating exhibits of fossils and minerals.

Uncover the secrets of our planet’s formation with engaging displays that reveal the processes shaping the Earth. This museum is a gateway to understanding geological time.

10:30 AM - Whipple Museum

Explore the evolution of scientific instruments and models at the Whipple Museum.

Dive into the fascinating world of science through artifacts that tell the story of human discovery. Learn about breakthroughs that changed the course of history.

12:00 PM - Mathematical Bridge

Marvel at the brilliant engineering behind the Mathematical Bridge at Queens’ College.

This remarkable structure combines art and mathematics in a seamless design. Learn about the engineering marvels behind its construction and the stories that surround it.

1:30 PM - Free Time (Lunch/Leisure)

Enjoy a relaxed break to savor lunch and take in the local flavors.

Use this time to explore local dining options or simply rest. It’s the perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in Cambridge’s culinary scene.

3:00 PM - St. John's College & Bridge of Sighs

Explore the enchanting grounds of St. John's College and admire the romantic Bridge of Sighs.

This enchanting area is steeped in tradition and lore. Learn about the legends and history behind the college and its famed bridge.

4:00 PM - The Round Church

Visit one of Cambridge’s oldest landmarks, famed for its unique circular design.

The Round Church offers insights into medieval architecture and religious history. Discover its origins, cultural significance, and enduring mysteries.

5:30 PM - Evensong at King's College Chapel

Experience the transcendent choral Evensong in the majestic setting of King's College Chapel.

Let the ethereal music and awe-inspiring Gothic architecture transport you. Learn about the chapel’s rich history, its role in Cambridge culture, and the power of its acoustics.

7:00 PM - Corpus Clock

Marvel at the innovative Corpus Clock—a modern icon that redefines the passage of time.

This striking timepiece embodies the beauty and impermanence of time. Learn about its design inspiration and the philosophy behind its mesmerizing display.

Saturday, 22 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Check-Out from Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Bid farewell to your elegant home base as you check out after an inspiring stay.

Reflect on the comfort and luxury you enjoyed during your stay. This smooth departure marks the end of an unforgettable Cambridge experience.

11:30 AM - Farewell Breakfast at Fitzbillies

Savor a classic farewell breakfast at the iconic Fitzbillies.

Enjoy the famous Chelsea buns and traditional fare that have delighted visitors for generations. This heartfelt meal is a perfect send-off from Cambridge.

1:00 PM - Last Stroll Through The Backs

Take one final, reflective walk along the scenic River Cam.

Absorb the tranquil beauty of The Backs one last time before you depart. This peaceful stroll is the perfect finale to your memorable journey.

2:00 PM - Departure from Cambridge

Bid farewell to Cambridge with cherished memories and inspiration for your next adventure.

Carry the beauty and insights of Cambridge with you as you leave. Your journey here will remain a cherished part of your travel story.
