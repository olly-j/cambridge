9:00 AM - Sedgwick Museum

Discover Earth’s ancient history through captivating exhibits of fossils and minerals.

Uncover the secrets of our planet’s formation with interactive displays that reveal geological processes. This museum is a portal to understanding the Earth's ancient past.

10:30 AM - Whipple Museum

Explore the evolution of scientific instruments and models at the Whipple Museum.

Discover fascinating artifacts that trace the journey of scientific discovery. Learn about breakthrough inventions that revolutionized our understanding of the natural world.

Learn about its medieval origins and religious significance. The Round Church remains an enduring symbol of Cambridge’s rich heritage.
