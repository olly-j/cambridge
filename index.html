Thursday, 20 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Arrival & Morning Exploration

Arrive in Cambridge and begin your adventure with an inspiring morning walk through its charming streets.

Explore the city on foot as you soak in the vibrant atmosphere and historic architecture. This relaxed start sets the tone for a day full of discovery and inspiration.

12:00 PM - Explore Trinity College

Wander through the storied halls and lush courtyards of Trinity College.

Trinity College is a cornerstone of Cambridge’s academic heritage. Discover its rich history, impressive architecture, and the traditions that have shaped generations of scholars.

1:00 PM - Lunch at Mercado Central (Booked)

Savor a delicious Spanish lunch at Mercado Central in a vibrant, bustling setting.

Enjoy the authentic flavors and lively ambiance that make Mercado Central a culinary gem. Learn about the signature dishes and ingredients that set this dining experience apart.

3:00 PM - Check-In at Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Relax and refresh as you settle into your elegant accommodation.

Experience modern comfort blended with historic charm. The Graduate provides an inspiring and luxurious setting to unwind before the afternoon’s adventures.

4:30 PM - Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

Explore centuries of art and history at the world-renowned Fitzwilliam Museum.

Discover diverse collections ranging from ancient artifacts to modern masterpieces. The museum provides profound insights into art, culture, and the evolution of human creativity.

6:00 PM - Stroll Along The Backs

Enjoy a scenic walk along the River Cam, admiring picturesque college gardens.

The Backs offer a stunning blend of nature and history. Learn about the heritage of the surrounding colleges while soaking in breathtaking views along the river.

7:30 PM - Evening Leisure Walk

Wind down your day with a reflective stroll through Cambridge’s softly lit streets.

Experience the enchanting transformation of the city at dusk. This peaceful walk offers a moment of quiet reflection, leaving you inspired by Cambridge’s magic.

Friday, 21 Feb 2025

9:00 AM - Sedgwick Museum

Discover Earth’s ancient history through captivating exhibits of fossils and minerals.

Uncover the secrets of our planet’s formation with interactive displays that reveal the processes shaping the Earth. This museum serves as a fascinating portal into geological time.

10:30 AM - Whipple Museum

Explore the evolution of scientific instruments and models at the Whipple Museum.

Discover fascinating artifacts that trace the journey of scientific innovation. Learn about the breakthroughs that transformed our understanding of the world.

12:00 PM - Mathematical Bridge

Marvel at the ingenious engineering of the Mathematical Bridge at Queens’ College.

This remarkable structure fuses mathematics and art, offering a captivating study in design. Discover the fascinating engineering behind this timeless marvel.

1:30 PM - Free Time (Lunch/Leisure)

Enjoy a relaxed break to savor lunch and explore local dining options.

Take time to recharge while experiencing the culinary delights of Cambridge. This break allows you to absorb the local flavors and ambiance.

3:00 PM - St. John's College & Bridge of Sighs

Explore the enchanting grounds of St. John's College and admire the romantic Bridge of Sighs.

This area is steeped in tradition and romance. Discover the legends behind the Bridge of Sighs and learn about the college’s rich cultural heritage.

4:00 PM - The Round Church

Visit one of Cambridge’s oldest landmarks, renowned for its unique circular design.

Learn about the medieval origins and religious significance of this rare architectural gem. Its design and history make it an enduring symbol of Cambridge.

5:30 PM - Evensong at King's College Chapel

Experience a transcendent choral performance in the majestic setting of King's College Chapel.

Let the ethereal music and awe-inspiring Gothic architecture transport you. Discover the chapel’s storied past and its continuing role in Cambridge’s cultural life.

7:00 PM - Corpus Clock

Marvel at the innovative Corpus Clock—a modern icon that redefines the passage of time.

Discover the philosophy and design behind this striking timepiece. Its contemporary aesthetic invites reflection on the transient nature of time.

Saturday, 22 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Check-Out from Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Bid farewell to your elegant home base as you check out after an inspiring stay.

Reflect on the comfort and luxury you experienced during your stay. This smooth departure marks the end of an unforgettable Cambridge adventure.

11:30 AM - Farewell Breakfast at Fitzbillies

Savor a classic farewell breakfast at the iconic Fitzbillies.

Enjoy the famous Chelsea buns and traditional fare that have delighted visitors for generations. This heartfelt meal is a perfect send-off from Cambridge.

1:00 PM - Last Stroll Through The Backs

Enjoy one final, reflective walk along the scenic River Cam.

Absorb the tranquil beauty of The Backs one last time before you depart. This peaceful stroll offers a perfect finale to your memorable journey.

2:00 PM - Departure from Cambridge

Bid farewell to Cambridge with cherished memories and inspiration for your next adventure.

Carry the beauty and insights of Cambridge with you as you leave. Your journey here will continue to inspire your future travels.
