Thursday, 20 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Arrival & Morning Exploration

Arrive in Cambridge and kick off your adventure with a scenic walk through its historic streets.

Start your day by immersing yourself in the city's vibrant atmosphere. Enjoy a coffee at a local café and take in the architecture that tells the story of Cambridge.

12:00 PM - Explore Trinity College

Wander through the storied halls and lush courtyards of Trinity College.

Discover the legacy of one of Cambridge’s most prestigious colleges. Learn about its rich history, impressive architecture, and the traditions that continue to inspire scholars.

Visit Trinity College

1:00 PM - Lunch at Mercado Central (Booked)

Enjoy a delicious Spanish lunch in a vibrant setting.

Mercado Central offers an authentic culinary experience. Savor unique dishes crafted with passion and learn about the fusion of traditional and modern Spanish flavors.

Visit Mercado Central

3:00 PM - Check-In at Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Settle into your elegant accommodation and relax before an exciting afternoon.

The Graduate blends modern comfort with historic charm. Unwind in luxurious surroundings that set the perfect tone for your journey.

Visit Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

4:30 PM - Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

Explore a world of art and antiquities at the Fitzwilliam Museum.

From ancient artifacts to modern masterpieces, the museum's collections offer a deep dive into the evolution of art and culture. Discover inspiring stories from every era.

Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

6:00 PM - Stroll Along The Backs

Enjoy a serene walk along the River Cam with picturesque views.

The Backs provide a stunning blend of natural beauty and historic architecture. Take a moment to reflect on the centuries of academic and cultural legacy that line the river.

View The Backs

7:30 PM - Evening Leisure Walk

Wind down your day with a reflective stroll through softly lit Cambridge streets.

Experience the enchanting transformation of Cambridge as dusk falls. Let the gentle glow of streetlights and historic buildings create a calming, magical end to your day.

Friday, 21 Feb 2025

9:00 AM - Sedgwick Museum

Discover Earth’s ancient history through fascinating exhibits of fossils and minerals.

Uncover the secrets of our planet’s formation with interactive displays that bring geological time to life. Learn how natural processes have shaped the Earth over millions of years.

Visit Sedgwick Museum

10:30 AM - Whipple Museum

Explore the evolution of scientific instruments and models at the Whipple Museum.

Delve into the history of innovation with artifacts that showcase humanity’s journey of discovery. Learn about the breakthroughs that transformed our understanding of science.

Visit Whipple Museum

12:00 PM - Mathematical Bridge

Marvel at the brilliant engineering behind the Mathematical Bridge at Queens’ College.

This iconic structure fuses art and mathematics in perfect harmony. Discover the engineering marvels that make this bridge a celebrated landmark.

Visit Mathematical Bridge

1:30 PM - Free Time (Lunch/Leisure)

Enjoy a relaxed break to savor lunch and explore local dining options.

Use this time to immerse yourself in Cambridge’s culinary scene. It’s the perfect opportunity to relax and reflect on the morning’s explorations.

3:00 PM - St. John's College & Bridge of Sighs

Explore the enchanting grounds of St. John's College and admire the romantic Bridge of Sighs.

Steeped in tradition and romance, this landmark invites you to learn about its storied past and the legends that give it its name.

Visit St. John's College

4:00 PM - The Round Church

Visit one of Cambridge’s oldest landmarks, renowned for its unique circular design.

Learn about the medieval origins and religious significance of this rare architectural gem. Its intriguing design tells a story of ancient faith and ingenuity.

Visit The Round Church

5:30 PM - Evensong at King's College Chapel

Experience the transcendent choral Evensong in the majestic setting of King's College Chapel.

Let the ethereal music and stunning Gothic architecture transport you. Discover the chapel’s history and its enduring impact on Cambridge’s cultural and spiritual life.

Visit King's College Chapel

7:00 PM - Corpus Clock

Marvel at the innovative Corpus Clock—a modern icon that redefines the passage of time.

Explore the striking design and underlying philosophy of this contemporary timepiece. It challenges your perception of time with its bold aesthetic.

Visit Corpus Clock

Saturday, 22 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Check-Out from Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Bid farewell to your elegant home base as you check out after an inspiring stay.

Reflect on the comfort and luxury you experienced during your stay. This smooth departure marks the end of a remarkable Cambridge journey.

11:30 AM - Farewell Breakfast at Fitzbillies

Savor a classic farewell breakfast at the iconic Fitzbillies.

Enjoy the famous Chelsea buns and traditional fare that have delighted visitors for generations. This heartfelt meal is a perfect send-off from Cambridge.

Visit Fitzbillies

1:00 PM - Last Stroll Through The Backs

Enjoy one final, reflective walk along the scenic River Cam.

Absorb the tranquil beauty of The Backs one last time before you depart. Let the peaceful views leave you with lasting memories.

View The Backs

2:00 PM - Departure from Cambridge

Bid farewell to Cambridge with cherished memories and inspiration for your next adventure.

Carry the beauty and insights of Cambridge with you as you leave. Your journey here will continue to inspire your future travels.
