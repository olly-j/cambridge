Thursday, 20 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Arrival & Morning Exploration

Begin your day with a scenic walk through Cambridge’s historic streets.

Immerse yourself in the city’s vibrant atmosphere and explore its hidden gems.

12:00 PM - Explore Trinity College

Stroll through the storied halls and lush courtyards of Trinity College.

Discover centuries of academic excellence and admire the breathtaking architecture.

Visit Trinity College

1:00 PM - Lunch at Mercado Central (Booked)

Savor a delicious Spanish lunch in a lively setting.

Experience authentic flavors with a fusion of traditional and modern culinary techniques.

Visit Mercado Central

3:00 PM - Check-In at Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Settle into your elegant accommodation and relax before the afternoon’s adventures.

Enjoy modern luxury intertwined with historic charm—a perfect prelude to your exploration.

Visit Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

4:30 PM - Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

Explore world-class art and antiquities at the Fitzwilliam Museum.

Discover diverse collections that span centuries, offering deep insights into art and culture.

Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

6:00 PM - Stroll Along The Backs

Enjoy a scenic walk along the River Cam, admiring picturesque college gardens.

The Backs offer a serene blend of nature and history—experience Cambridge in its purest form.

View Map

7:30 PM - Evening Leisure Walk

Wind down your day with a reflective stroll through softly lit Cambridge streets.

Let the enchanting evening ambiance create a serene conclusion to your day.

Friday, 21 Feb 2025

9:00 AM - Sedgwick Museum

Discover ancient history through captivating exhibits of fossils and minerals.

Interact with displays that reveal the Earth’s formative processes and geological secrets.

Visit Sedgwick Museum

10:30 AM - Whipple Museum

Explore the evolution of scientific instruments and models.

Discover artifacts that trace the journey of scientific innovation and discovery.

Visit Whipple Museum

12:00 PM - Mathematical Bridge

Marvel at the brilliant engineering behind the Mathematical Bridge at Queens’ College.

Experience the perfect fusion of art and mathematics in a celebrated landmark.

Visit Mathematical Bridge

1:30 PM - Free Time (Lunch/Leisure)

Enjoy a relaxed break to savor lunch and explore local dining options.

Recharge and immerse yourself in Cambridge’s culinary scene for a taste of local flavor.

3:00 PM - St. John's College & Bridge of Sighs

Explore the enchanting grounds and the romantic Bridge of Sighs at St. John's College.

Learn about its storied past and the legends that make this landmark truly unforgettable.

Visit St. John's College

4:00 PM - The Round Church

Visit one of Cambridge’s oldest landmarks, renowned for its unique circular design.

Explore its medieval origins and religious significance, a rare gem in Cambridge’s architectural history.

Visit The Round Church

5:30 PM - Evensong at King's College Chapel

Experience a transcendent choral performance in the majestic setting of King's College Chapel.

Let the ethereal music and Gothic splendor transport you through centuries of tradition and culture.

Visit King's College Chapel

7:00 PM - Corpus Clock

Marvel at the innovative Corpus Clock—a modern icon that redefines time.

Explore its striking design and underlying philosophy that challenges the very nature of time.

View Map

Saturday, 22 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Check-Out

Depart from the Graduate by Hilton Cambridge.

Reflect on a stay filled with luxury and inspiration as you say farewell to this historic city.

11:30 AM - Farewell Breakfast

Savor a classic Cambridge breakfast at Fitzbillies.

Indulge in renowned Chelsea buns and traditional fare—a heartfelt send-off to your Cambridge adventure.

Visit Fitzbillies

1:00 PM - Last Stroll Through The Backs

Enjoy one final, reflective walk along the scenic River Cam.

Absorb the tranquil beauty of The Backs one last time before you depart, leaving with memories to last a lifetime.

View Map

2:00 PM - Departure

Bid farewell to Cambridge with cherished memories and inspiration for your next adventure.

Carry the beauty and insights of this historic city with you as you embark on your next journey.
