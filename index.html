Thursday, 20 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Arrival & Morning Exploration

Arrive in Cambridge and begin your adventure with a scenic walk through its historic streets.

Immerse yourself in the city's vibrant atmosphere as you explore local cafés and soak in the architectural wonders of Cambridge.

12:00 PM - Explore Trinity College

Wander through the storied halls and lush courtyards of Trinity College.

Discover its rich history and academic legacy while admiring the impressive architecture that inspires scholars worldwide.

Visit Trinity College

1:00 PM - Lunch at Mercado Central (Booked)

Savor a delicious Spanish lunch in a vibrant, bustling setting.

Enjoy authentic flavors and discover the fusion of traditional and modern culinary techniques that make this dining experience unique.

Visit Mercado Central

3:00 PM - Check-In at Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Settle into your elegant accommodation and relax before the afternoon’s adventures.

Experience modern comfort intertwined with historic charm in a setting designed to inspire and rejuvenate.

Visit Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

4:30 PM - Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

Explore centuries of art and history at the world-renowned Fitzwilliam Museum.

From ancient artifacts to modern masterpieces, immerse yourself in diverse collections that chronicle human creativity.

Visit Fitzwilliam Museum

6:00 PM - Stroll Along The Backs

Enjoy a scenic walk along the River Cam, admiring picturesque college gardens.

The Backs offer a stunning blend of nature and history, where you can reflect on centuries of academic and cultural legacy.

View The Backs Map

7:30 PM - Evening Leisure Walk

Wind down your day with a reflective stroll through Cambridge’s softly lit streets.

Let the enchanting evening ambiance and historic architecture inspire a moment of quiet reflection.

Friday, 21 Feb 2025

9:00 AM - Sedgwick Museum

Discover Earth’s ancient history through fascinating exhibits of fossils and minerals.

Explore interactive displays that reveal geological processes and uncover the secrets of our planet’s formation.

Visit Sedgwick Museum

10:30 AM - Whipple Museum

Explore the evolution of scientific instruments and models at the Whipple Museum.

Discover fascinating artifacts that trace the journey of scientific discovery and groundbreaking innovation.

Visit Whipple Museum

12:00 PM - Mathematical Bridge

Marvel at the brilliant engineering behind the Mathematical Bridge at Queens’ College.

This iconic structure fuses art and mathematics—discover its innovative design and the stories that make it legendary.

Visit Mathematical Bridge

1:30 PM - Free Time (Lunch/Leisure)

Enjoy a relaxed break to savor lunch and explore local dining options.

Recharge with a leisurely lunch—immerse yourself in the local culinary scene and reflect on your morning explorations.

3:00 PM - St. John's College & Bridge of Sighs

Explore the enchanting grounds of St. John's College and admire the romantic Bridge of Sighs.

Steeped in tradition and romance, this landmark invites you to learn about its storied past and the legends behind the famous bridge.

Visit St. John's College

4:00 PM - The Round Church

Visit one of Cambridge’s oldest landmarks, famed for its unique circular design.

Learn about its medieval origins and religious significance—a rare architectural gem that reflects Cambridge’s ancient history.

Visit The Round Church

5:30 PM - Evensong at King's College Chapel

Experience a transcendent choral performance in the majestic setting of King's College Chapel.

Let the ethereal music and stunning Gothic architecture transport you. Discover the chapel’s rich history and its lasting cultural impact.

Visit King's College Chapel

7:00 PM - Corpus Clock

Marvel at the innovative Corpus Clock—a modern icon that redefines time.

Explore its striking design and underlying philosophy that challenge your perception of time.

View Corpus Clock Map

Saturday, 22 Feb 2025

11:00 AM - Check-Out from Graduate by Hilton Cambridge

Bid farewell to your elegant home base after an inspiring stay.

Reflect on the comfort and luxury you experienced during your stay as you prepare to conclude your Cambridge journey.

11:30 AM - Farewell Breakfast at Fitzbillies

Savor a classic farewell breakfast at the iconic Fitzbillies.

Enjoy the famous Chelsea buns and traditional fare that have delighted visitors for generations—a warm send-off from Cambridge.

Visit Fitzbillies

1:00 PM - Last Stroll Through The Backs

Enjoy one final, reflective walk along the scenic River Cam.

Absorb the tranquil beauty of The Backs one last time before you depart. Let the peaceful views create lasting memories of your journey.

View The Backs Map

2:00 PM - Departure from Cambridge

Bid farewell to Cambridge with cherished memories and inspiration for your next adventure.

Carry the beauty and insights of this historic city with you as you leave, and let the experience inspire your future travels.
